FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my work process

What is your design process?

My design process involves understanding your needs, creating concepts, refining the design, and delivering the final product. I keep you involved throughout each stage.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary by complexity. A simple website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects might take 3-6 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial discussion.

Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, I provide ongoing support and maintenance to ensure your website stays current and functions perfectly after launch.

How do you handle revisions?

I include revision rounds in the project scope. My iterative approach ensures you can provide feedback at each stage of the design process.

What information do you need for a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, I need to understand your project goals, scope, timeline, target audience, and any specific requirements. I'll help you outline these during our initial consultation.
